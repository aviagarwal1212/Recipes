## Baked Chicken

### Marinade

* Ginger garlic paste
* Lemon juice
* Paprika
* Rosemary
* Honey
* A little bit of cumin powder

Before putting the chicken in the marinade, season it with salt and pepper. 

### Procedure

* Marinate for 1-2 hours (as long as possible)
* Bake 10 min @ 250C, then 10-15min @ 200C
